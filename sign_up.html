Your username must be 3-25 characters long, start with a letter (a-z), and can only contain letters, numbers, and one optional symbol (_ - .) in the middle. Examples: 'User123', 'name-test', or 'first.last'. Symbols cannot be at the start/end or consecutive.

Please enter your email address starting with a letter, containing only letters, numbers, periods (.), underscores (_) or hyphens (-), and ending with @gmail.com or @icloud.com. The username must be 3-20 characters long (e.g., john.doe123@gmail.com or jane_smith@icloud.com). Special symbols can't be at the start/end or consecutive.

Create a password between 8-20 characters that contains at least one letter, one number, and one special symbol (@, $, !, %, , #, ?, or &). For example: 'Summer@2024' or 'MyPassw0rd!' meets these requirements.
